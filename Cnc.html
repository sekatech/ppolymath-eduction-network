Current Competitive Examinations

Public Health Professionals

Aptitude Test for registration in the National Order of Health Professionals for holders of HND, BTS, HPD, DSEP, or Bachelor's degrees in Health Sciences.

Deadline: 21st July 2025 South West Region

Eligibility:

  • Cameroonian nationality
  • HND/BTS, HPD/DSEP, or BSc/Licence in Health Sciences

Exam Dates:

Written: 9th August 2025
Practical: 20-23 August 2025

Customs Officers Recruitment

Direct competitive examination for recruitment of 120 personnel into the Revenue Services (Customs) civil servants.

Deadline: 4th July 2025 Nationwide

Positions:

  • 60 Assistant Controllers (Category C)
  • 60 Clerical Assistants (Category D)

Requirements:

Category C: GCE O/L or BEPC
Category D: FSLC or CEP

Exam Dates: 19-20 July 2025

GTTC Entrance Examination

Competitive examination for admission into Government Teacher Training Colleges (GTTC) nationwide.

Deadline: 7th July 2025 6,130 places available

Requirements:

  • Age 17-32 years
  • GCE Advanced Level or equivalent

GTTC Types:

  • Traditional (3,220 places)
  • Bilingual (2,570 places)
  • Specialized (340 places)

FET Engineering Entrance

Competitive entrance examination into the Faculty of Engineering and Technology (FET) at University of Buea.

Deadline: 22nd Sept 2025 520 places available

Programs:

  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Chemical/Petroleum Eng.
  • Electrical/Electronic Eng.
  • Mechanical Engineering

Requirements:

GCE O/L (5 subjects incl. Math, Physics) + A/L (Math +1)
